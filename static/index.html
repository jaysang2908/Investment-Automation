Investment Utopia

Enter any ticker. The engine fetches financials from FMP, runs WACC and a DCF, scores 11 criteria (with manual option for 2 qualitative factors below) and generates a full Excel Model + HTML report.

Not investment advice. All outputs should be verified manually by the user as AI automation is not always reliable or accurate. Certain default rules exist and the user is encouraged to change inputs in the DCF model/calculator in particular to test potential valuation boundaries. Scorecard optimisation is still WIP as some sectors require specific calibration of metrics (i.e. banks are currently scoring low due to incorrect application of risk and credit metrics).

Leaving both fields blank caps the score at 87.5 / 100 (financial metrics only) — verdict thresholds adjust accordingly.

How We Analyse

01
Business Quality

Moat, management track record, long-term positioning — proxy-scored from financials, refined manually from 10-K.

02
Financial Rigour

Five-year P&L, balance sheet, and cash flow — auto-fetched from FMP. ROIC, FCF conversion, D/EBITDA, EBIT/interest fully computed.

03
Valuation

WACC (Damodaran) + DCF (Gordon Growth + Exit Multiple) + sensitivity grid. Multiples vs. 5-year averages. Bear / Base / Bull scenarios.

04
Risk & Credit

Credit ratings, interest coverage, leverage, execution risks — weighted into the 0–100 scorecard. 11 of 13 criteria auto-scored.
